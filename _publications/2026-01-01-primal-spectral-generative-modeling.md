This paper studies primal-to-spectral generative modeling and introduces a fast analytical generation framework based on Pseudoinverse Levy Inversion. The proposed PriSpecNet architecture turns generation into a closed-form evaluation problem, enabling efficient one-step sampling while remaining compatible with stochastic interpolation.
