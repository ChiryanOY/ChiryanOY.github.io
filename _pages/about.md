I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computational Mathematics at the [School of Mathematical Sciences, East China Normal University](https://math.ecnu.edu.cn/) and the [Shanghai Institute of Intelligence](https://www.sii.edu.cn/), co-supervised by [Assoc. Prof. Xiangyun Zhang](https://math.ecnu.edu.cn/~xyzhang/) and [Prof. Junchi Yan](https://soai.sjtu.edu.cn/cn/facultydetails/zzjs/yanjunchi). My research centers on generative models, with an emphasis on theoretical foundations, generalization, interpretability, and low-dimensional structure discovery for efficient, controllable, and safe generation.

Beyond research, I work comfortably across the AI engineering stack, including Slurm-based cluster scheduling and DDP distributed training at large scale. I am also deeply interested in Web3 systems and prediction markets, especially on-chain quantitative trading infrastructure around Polymarket.
